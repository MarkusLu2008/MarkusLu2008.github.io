Managing Social Virtual Group Members
for Hong Kong Youth Services using a Discord Bot

Markus is the Head Developer for this project, which serves the “6PM Cyber Youth Support Team” of the non-profit organization “St. Jame’s Settlement” in Hong Kong.

1. Introduction

Hikikomori (hidden youth) is a serious issue in Hong Kong. According to Wong, up to 2% of Hong Kong's population might be classified as withdrawn. Paul claims that there are hidden youngsters among Hong Kong's 140,000 young people. (Durston, 2019) In addition, COVID-19 has caused Hongkongers to live in social isolation for the first time.

Social service councils in Hong Kong like St. James' Settlement offer hidden youngsters a social forum to communicate with each other, and social workers will hold some events to motivate teenagers to be positive. 6PM Cyber Youth Support Team is a government-funded services team which conducts youth outreach through online medium. The nonprofit organisation St James Settlement‘s social workers provide support and counselling, via both online and offline means, to at-risk and hidden youth. 6PM has a Discord server for hidden youngsters to communicate. It is a heavy task to manage a social forum; some enthusiasts develop a bot that can help social workers manage the social forum more efficiently. However, most chatbots for social service councils are not powerful. We would like to develop a Management System with Discord bot to help the administrator and social workers in SJS to manage the Discord users more easily.

Project Aim and Objectives

This project aims to develop a Discord bot and a web application for managing

  • Enhances the membership registration system in Discord
  • Collects and stores the membership information by using Discord bot
  • Provides training to staff on behalf of management to enhance long-term service quality and productivity

Markus' Tasks

  • Communicates with staff to gain insight on the existing workflow and project goals
  • Enhances the membership data collection process to improve service efficiency
  • Develops a bot for membership information collection and management
  • Evaluates with staff and provides training to ensure the sustainability of the improved workflow
